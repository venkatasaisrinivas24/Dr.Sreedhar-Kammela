Why Choose Us

Advanced Treatments, Compassionate Care

We are dedicated to providing comprehensive and advanced solutions in infertility, kidney care, and multi-specialty obstetrics and gynecology. Our commitment is to offer world-class medical care with a personalized and compassionate touch, ensuring the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Pioneering & Recognized Expertise

With a legacy of "Firsts" in South India, including the introduction of advanced treatments like the Pixel CO2 Laser and ERECTILE DYSFUNCTION SHOCKWAVE THERAPY (EDST), our hospital stands at the forefront of medical innovation. Our doctors are not only practitioners but also recipients of numerous prestigious awards for their contributions to medicine, including multiple World Records, '5' National Awards, and '11' World Records, reflecting our vast experience and commitment to excellence.

Highly Qualified & Awarded Specialists

Our team is led by internationally recognized experts, Dr. Kammela Sreedhar and Dr. Kammela Sridevi, who hold fellowships and advanced training from prestigious institutions in the USA and Europe. Their expertise in robotic surgery, advanced laparoscopy, and high-risk obstetric management, combined with accolades like the "Vaidya Ratna Award," ensures you receive care from distinguished leaders in the medical field.

Comprehensive Multi-Specialty Diagnostics

We offer a holistic approach to diagnosis and treatment under one roof, covering a wide spectrum of conditions in Urology, Andrology, Gynecology, and Infertility. From detailed infertility investigations for both partners to advanced obstetric sonography and urodynamic studies, we utilize state-of-the-art diagnostic tools to ensure an accurate and thorough analysis for every patient.

Patient-Centric & Accessible Care

Your comfort and care are our top priorities. We provide a supportive environment with timings designed to accommodate your schedule, including evening hours. With two convenient locations in Mehdipatnam and Gachibowli, and doctors who actively engage with the community through live television health programs, we are dedicated to being an accessible and trusted healthcare partner on your journey to wellness.
